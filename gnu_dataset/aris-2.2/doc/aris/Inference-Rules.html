6.1 Inference Rules

The premises of any of these rules can be in any order.

• mp:  Modus Ponens
• ad:  Addition
• sm:  Simplification
• cn:  Conjunction
• hs:  Hypothetical Syllogism
• ds:  Disjunctive Syllogism
• ex:  Excluded Middle
• cd:  Constructive Dilemma
