6.3 Predicate Rules

The predicate rules are the rules that work specifically with predicate logic.

• ug:  Universal Generalization
• ui:  Universal Instantiation
• eg:  Existential Generalization
• ei:  Existential Instantiation
• bv:  Bound Variable
• nq:  Null Quantifier
• pr:  Prenex
• ii:  Identity
• fv:  Free Variable
